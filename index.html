Tech radar

The Radar is a document that describes the technology landscape as applied at Administrate.
It is a knowledge base about where we are today as a company and where we want to go.

Aim of the tech radar

  • Centralize knowledge of what our favoured tech stack is.
  • Provides opinion and guidance on new technology frontiers.
  • Captures relevant decisions on the changing technology landscape.

the original concept comes from thoughtworks, you can read more about it here.

Drop
